This is the visual and verbal identity of "Project Reflect."
1. Color PaletteYour palette is our guide. It's warm, soft, and sophisticated. We'll use Tailwind's extend feature to create these.
Primary Background: bg-peach-50 (A very light, warm, almost-white peach).
Content Backgrounds: bg-white or bg-peach-100 (For cards, text boxes).
Primary Interactive: bg-purple-600 (The "main" button color, like "Start Reflection"). 
Dark, rich, but inviting.Secondary Interactive: bg-mauve-400 (For less important buttons 
or highlights).Accent/Highlight: bg-peach-400 (For icons or active states).
Text (Standard): text-purple-900 (A very dark, soft purple, not harsh black).
Text (Muted): text-mauve-700 (For descriptions, sub-headings).Text (Button): text-white 
or text-peach-100.2. TypographyFont: We'll import a Google Font. "Nunito" is a perfect 
choice. It's rounded, friendly, and highly legible. It feels modern and gentle, not 
"threatening" or corporate.Headings (e.g., "Your Daily Reflection"): font-nunito text-2xl
 font-bold text-purple-900Body Text: font-nunito text-lg text-purple-800 (Note: We'll 
 use text-lg as the default for an easy-to-read, mobile-first experience).3. Verbiage
